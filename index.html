About Me

I'm a versatile IT professional from Accra, Ghana, with a passion for both technology and creative design. With a strong foundation in networking administration, database management, and cybersecurity, I ensure robust and secure digital infrastructures.

As a graduate of Computer Science from the University of Cape Coast (UCC), my expertise extends to machine learning, where I develop intelligent solutions, and to 3D visualization and cinematography, where I create stunning visual experiences. I'm also proficient in Adobe Office Suite and web design tools like Figma.

Fluent in English, Twi, and Ga, I bring a multicultural perspective to every project and can effectively communicate with diverse teams and clients.

What sets me apart is my ability to bridge the gap between technical excellence and creative innovation, delivering solutions that are both functional and visually appealing.
